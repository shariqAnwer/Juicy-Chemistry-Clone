How do I use it?

STEP 1:

Rub the soap between your palms and work up a lather.



STEP 2:

Spread lather on your wet face and body with gentle strokes.



STEP 3:

Scrub your body gently with JC's Bath Mitt (twice a week only).



STEP 4:

Wash off with lukewarm water.



STEP 5:

Pat dry with a soft towel and apply your favourite JC moisturiser.

Why these ingredients?

Lime essential oil acts as a natural antioxidant and antimicrobial agent. It helps improve skin health and promotes a more even tone.

Ginger essential oil exhibits strong antioxidant properties, helps reduce oxidative stress, and calms skin inflammation. 

Rice extract helps maintain skin texture, suppleness, and hydration. Its antioxidant content promotes healthier and more youthful skin.

Why this source?

Our Lime Essential Oil is sourced from India because it provides the ideal loamy soil alongside a warm and moist climate which is most conducive to the growth of limes. 

Our Ginger Essential Oil comes from India because the growth of Ginger is well-suited to the warm and humid climate of the country.

Our Rice Water comes from India because it is native to this country and provides the most conducive climate for rice crops.

Why this formulation?

Essential Fatty Acids make this soap bar super-fatted, which allows it to replenish the skin lipids compromised during cleansing. 

Antioxidants like Phytic acid, Ferulic Acid, Zingiberene, and Camphene, protect skin against free radicals and promote a more even skin tone. Amino Acids help keep the skin supple, nourished, and smooth. 

Polyphenols protect skin against sun damage, promote a more even skin tone, and soothe inflammation.

Plant Vitamins like Vitamin A, C, and E help keep the skin hydrated, supple, and bright. 

Alpha - Hydroxy Acids like phytic acid help promote healthy and smooth skin. 

Minerals like montmorillonite help deep clean pores, while also absorbing all impurities and sebum from the skin.
